The Story

From Chicago's South Side
to Celebrity Kitchens

Chef Christina Gray's culinary journey began on Chicago's South Side, where she first experimented with cooking for friends and family. Her talent flourished during a stint in broadcast radio, where she delighted coworkers with her dishes, eventually shifting her focus from the airwaves to the kitchen full-time in 2013.

Her drive to elevate her craft brought her to Los Angeles, where she quickly built a reputation as the go-to private chef for entertainment's elite. From intimate dinners for NBA All-Stars to yacht catering for BET Weekend and Black Yacht Week, Chef Tina became known for dishes that blend bold flavors with impeccable presentation.

Beyond the celebrity kitchen, she's championed causes close to her heart, advocating for underprivileged children in Uganda and exploring high-end American-fusion dining in Ghana. Her nonprofit, The Ruby Foundation, transforms lives through culinary excellence and community engagement.

In 2020, she turned the pandemic into opportunity, launching Razzle Dazzle, her artisan hot sauce and seasoning line. Today, she serves as Executive Event Chef for the Chicago Fire FC, feeding 60+ professional athletes daily while continuing to grow her brand partnerships and culinary legacy.

"I don't just cook for people. I create moments they remember."
- Chef Christina Gray
